Nakagawa: I am so excited! You are finally going to write some new stories for your famous manga series, Enjoy Detective!

Manga Artist: Ha! Well, I'm happy you'll be my editor again, Nakagawa-san. I trust you a lot and I look forward to working with you.

Nakagawa: About that...

Manga Artist: Hmm?

Nakagawa: Well, there's something I have to tell you. Kimura, can you come here please?

Kimura: I am honored to meet you, sir. I'm Kimura Taeko. I just graduated from university and joined this publishing company last week.

Manga Artist: What? This must be a joke, right? Are you telling me that...

Nakagawa: Yes, she will be your new editor.

Manga Artist: You have worked with me before and you have experience. I don't want to be impolite, but is she ready for a big project like this?

Kimura: Sir, I may be young with no experience, but I promise you that I will do my very best.

Manga Artist: Do you always speak so loudly?

Kimura: I'm sorry, sir.

Nakagawa: If there are any problems, I promise to help out.

Manga Artist: Nakagawa-san!

Manga Artist: Well then, Kimura-san, have you read the first draft?

Kimura: Of course, sir!

Manga Artist: Okay. What did you think?

Kimura: It was really good. I loved it. Only you could write that. How are they going to find the killer? I can't wait to read the next story.

Manga Artist: Hmm.

Kimura: But there is just one thing.

Nakagawa: Kimura! What are you talking about? Keep quiet!

Manga Artist: It's all right. What is it?

Kimura: The detective wears sunglasses.

Manga Artist: Yes? Why is that a problem?

Kimura: Why did you make him wear sunglasses?

Manga Artist: Huh?

Kimura: Do the sunglasses play an important role in the future? Are they connected with the murder?

Manga Artist: Uh, what do you mean?

Kimura: For example, are they a special type of sunglasses? Maybe they help you see in the dark and he's the killer?

Manga Artist: Uh, no, no. He is not the killer. The real killer is someone different.

Kimura: Oh. Then why is he wearing them?

Manga Artist: Do I need a reason? Sunglasses are cool.

Kimura: So, the detective is going to be someone who cares about fashion. Is that right?

Manga Artist: Uh, no. I was thinking he is someone who doesn't care about clothes or fashion. He only cares about work.

Kimura: Hmm. It doesn't make sense. People who don't care about fashion probably don't wear sunglasses.

Manga Artist: Oh, well, he doesn't like sunlight.

Kimura: Your story takes place inside a room with no windows.

Manga Artist: Uh...

Kimura: Sir, is it possible that you just didn't want to draw the eyes of the detective?

Nakagawa: Kimura! You should be careful with your words!

Manga Artist: You are exactly right. I'll take the sunglasses off.

Nakagawa: What? Kimura was right?

Manga Artist: Kimura-san, you have a good eye and you weren't scared to tell me your opinion. I like you. We are going to make a good team.

Kimura: Thank you, sir.
